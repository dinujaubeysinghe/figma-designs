# 🎨 Graphify — UI/UX Design

> A custom print-on-demand marketplace where users can buy personalized printed products and sellers can list items for sale.

---

## 📖 About the Project

**Graphify** is a print-on-demand e-commerce platform designed to connect buyers and sellers through a seamless and intuitive interface. Users can register, log in, and purchase custom-designed printed products such as t-shirts, bags, mugs, mats, and more — all with their own unique designs.

What makes Graphify special is its **dual-role system** — any user can switch their account into a seller account, allowing them to list and sell their own printed items. Buyers can personalize the same listed items with their own designs before purchasing.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 🔐 **User Authentication** — Simple and secure register and login flow
- 🛍️ **Product Marketplace** — Browse and buy custom printed products
- 🎨 **Custom Design Upload** — Users can apply their own designs to any product
- 👕 **Product Categories** — T-shirts, bags, mugs, mats, and more
- 🔄 **Seller Account Switch** — Any user can upgrade their account to a seller
- 🏪 **Seller Dashboard** — Sellers can list, manage, and sell their products
- 📦 **Order Management** — Track purchases and sales seamlessly
